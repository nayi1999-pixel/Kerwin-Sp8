About Kerwin

Building Better Leaders and Stronger Newsrooms

With over 20 years of experience, I am an award-winning television journalist with a strong track record of producing local news and programming. My background spans from leading editorial strategy at REVOLT Black News to serving as Managing Director of Spectrum News in Charlotte, NC.

I've earned an Emmy and an Edward R. Murrow Award for impactful storytelling. Today, as Poynter Faculty, I lead initiatives like the Mental Health Reporting Project and the Transforming Crime Coverage program, helping organizations build ethical frameworks and navigate the transforming media landscape with empathy and clarity.

Great leadership isn't about position. It's about purpose.
